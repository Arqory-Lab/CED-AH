## A Note on Recognition

This project distinguishes between contributions that *support* the framework and contributions that *challenge* it.  Both are valuable.  A well argued challenge that identifies a genuine weakness is a greater contribution than uncritical agreement.  Successful challengers receive explicit recognition because the framework's credibility depends on its willingness to be corrected.

If you identify an error and we revise the framework as a result, your contribution has made the entire project more coherent.  That deserves acknowledgment.
