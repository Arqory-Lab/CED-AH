## Two Separate Bodies of Work

The Arqory Research Institute maintains two distinct bodies of work.  Understanding the boundary between them is essential.

### This Repository: Open Safety Framework

This repository contains the CED-AH philosophical framework, safety standards, design imperatives, failure mode analysis, measurement protocols, and supporting evidence base.  This is public good research distributed under Apache License 2.0 (code) and Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (documentation).

**No patents are asserted over the contents of this repository.**  The ideas, frameworks, standards, and methodologies published here are freely available for use, modification, and redistribution under the terms of their respective licenses.

### Separate From This Repository: Proprietary Infrastructure Technology

The Arqory Research Institute and its affiliated commercial entity develop proprietary AI infrastructure technology that implements the safety principles described in this repository.  This technology, including patent pending architectures for multi-model orchestration, context management, compression, and reasoning is developed, maintained, and distributed separately.

**The proprietary technology is not present in this repository.**  It is not licensed under Apache 2.0 or CC BY 4.0. It is not available for inspection, use, or redistribution through this project.

## What This Patent Notice Clarifies

To eliminate ambiguity:

### Using This Repository Does NOT Grant Rights To Proprietary Technology

Using, forking, or contributing to the CED-AH open source project does not grant any license, express, implied, or by estoppel, to The Arqory's proprietary patent pending technology.  The Apache 2.0 applies to the contents of this repository and nothing else owned by The Arqory Research Institute or its affiliated commercial entity.

The safety frameworks described here can be implemented by anyone using any technology.  Our proprietary technology is one implementation among potentially many.  The open framework and the proprietary implementation are legally and architecturally independent.

### Contributing To This Repository Does NOT Affect Your Own Patents

Contributing to CED-AH grants a patent license pursuant to the terms of the (Apache 2.0 license and [CLA](CLA.md)).  It does not grant any broader license to your patent portfolio, does not affect patents unrelated to your contribution, and does not create any obligation beyond what is described in those agreements.

### The Arqory's Patents Do NOT Restrict Use of This Repository

The Arqory Research Institute commits that its patent holdings and pending applications will not be asserted against any person or entity for using the contents of this repository in accordance with the Apache 2.0 license.  The open source project and the proprietary technology occupy separate legal and technical domains.  One does not encumber the other.
